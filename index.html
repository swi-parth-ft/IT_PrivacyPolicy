Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 14, 2024

Welcome to Imagine Tales, a platform created to help families engage in imaginative storytelling. Your privacy is very important to us, and this Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect different types of information to provide and improve Imagine Tales:

a. Personal Information

Account Data: When you sign up as a parent or create a profile for your child, we collect basic information like your email address, name, and unique user identifiers.

Child Profiles: Parents may create child profiles, which include usernames and other non-identifiable information provided by the parent. These profiles are stored under the parent account.

b. User Content

Stories and Images: When children create stories and images, this content is stored and linked to the parent account. Parents have access to review and approve or deny content created by children.

c. Device Information

Device and Usage Data: We collect information about the device you use to access Imagine Tales to enhance app performance. This includes device type, operating system, and app activity logs.

d. Payment Information

Subscriptions and In-App Purchases: If you choose to subscribe or make in-app purchases, payment details are processed securely through App Store and Google Play, and we do not store payment information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the Imagine Tales app.
  • Personalize user experiences.
  • Manage subscriptions and story limits based on your plan.
  • Enable parental review and approval of child-created content.
  • Send notifications to parents and children for app activity, such as friend requests, story approvals, and likes.

3. Sharing of Information

We may share your information with third parties under the following conditions:

  • Service Providers: We work with trusted third parties for data processing, analytics, and subscriptions (e.g., RevenueCat for subscription management). These providers are obligated to protect your information.
  • Compliance and Legal Obligations: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to legal requests, to protect our users, or to enforce our policies.

4. Third-Party Services

Imagine Tales uses third-party services to enhance app functionality:

  • OpenAI API: We use OpenAI's API to assist with story generation based on user input. Information sent to OpenAI is limited to story prompts and selected character details, and is used solely to generate content within the app. You can review OpenAI's privacy practices in their Privacy Policy.
  • Gemini API: The Gemini API is also utilized for theme and genre customization features within the app. We ensure that data shared with Gemini is limited to the functionality of creating unique story content and does not include personally identifiable information. You can review Gemini's privacy practices in their Privacy Policy.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no online data transmission is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Parental Control

Imagine Tales provides parental control over children’s activities on the platform. Parents have the ability to review and approve or deny stories and can manage child profiles. We ask that parents oversee their children’s use of Imagine Tales to ensure a safe experience.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access and Update Information: Access and update your account details and child profiles.
  • Delete Information: Request deletion of your account and associated data.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at theparthantala@gmail.com.

8. Children’s Privacy

Imagine Tales is designed for children under parental supervision. We rely on parents to create and manage child accounts and profiles, which do not include personally identifiable information.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at theparthantala@gmail.com.

By using Imagine Tales, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.
